About

I am a Sr. Data Integrity Analyst at Atmos Energy, which is one of the largest natural gas only utility distributors in the US. I work in their Pipeline Safety Group, where I utilize data analytics tools such as Alteryx and Tableau to extract data from multiple compliance systems and create executive level dashboards to assist in decision making and ensure our pipelines are operating as safe as possible. I have also been learning how to program in Python and have a working proficiency in querying databases using SQL and completed my Master's in Artificial Intelligence at the University of North Texas in July 2023.

Previously, I worked as a Business Support Specialist and Financial Analyst at Atmos Energy where I was responsible for forecasting dollars spent on capital pipeline projects and creating operational/capital budgets for my business unit. I have a Bachelors degree in Economics with a Business minor from UT Austin, and a Masters degree in Accounting from UT Dallas. I am also a licensed CPA in the state of Texas and am well versed in accounting principles and business fundamentals.
